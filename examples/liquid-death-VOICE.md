## Brand Voice Guidelines

### ✅ Do
- Lean into the dark, unexpected, or provocative — that's the brand
- Use death, danger, and dramatic language intentionally and with confidence
- Mock corporate speak — it's part of the positioning
- Explain complex ideas in simple terms
- Keep sentences short and punchy — cut every unnecessary word
- Use numbers, data, and concrete examples
- Use 'you' and 'we' — talk to real humans

### ❌ Don't
- Sanitize or soften the edge — that kills the voice
- Sound like a normal beverage brand
- Use earnest, sincere marketing language without irony
- Hide behind jargon
- Write paragraphs when a sentence will do
- Make vague claims without backing them up
- Sound like a press release
